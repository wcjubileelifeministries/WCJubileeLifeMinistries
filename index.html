Who We Are

About West Coast Jubilee
Life Ministries

We are a Christ-centred church serving the Saldanha community with a heart for worship, discipleship, prayer, and meaningful connection. Our desire is to build lives, strengthen families, and share the love of God with everyone who walks through our doors.

Faith & Family Resources

Practical resources to help you and your household grow in faith — every day, not just on Sundays. Join our WhatsApp Community to receive them directly.

🏠

Family & Home Devotions

Weekly devotional posters and guided prayers designed for your home — a simple, meaningful way to keep God's Word at the centre of daily family life. Print them, display them, and let faith take root in your household.

  • Weekly devotional posters
  • Family prayer guides
  • Scripture focus cards
📚

Biblical Teaching & Faith Formation

Sermon notes, biblical teaching summaries, and doctrinal foundations explained in plain language. Whether you are new to faith or have walked with God for years, there is always more to discover and understand in His Word.
